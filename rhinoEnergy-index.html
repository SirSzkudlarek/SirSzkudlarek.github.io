Project:
Rhino Energy

Website done for Client.

I am a co-creator of the Rhino Energy website. I participated in the project while working as a Front-End Developer at WebVersum. I made over 25+ pages and their various language versions.

The website has been coded from scratch without using a page builder. Wordpress was used for content management "CMS". Thanks to this, the client can independently influence the content on the website. The website is fully optimized for mobile devices, tablets and desktop users. Tailwind was used to stylize the website.
